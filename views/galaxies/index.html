Galaxies

What Are Galaxies?

Galaxies are huge collections of gas, dust, and billions of stars, all held together by gravity. They come in different shapes and sizes and fill up our universe!

Fun Facts About Galaxies

  • ✨ There are over 2 trillion galaxies in the observable universe!
  • ✨ Our home, the Milky Way, is a spiral galaxy.
  • ✨ Some galaxies are larger than 1 million light-years across.
  • ✨ Galaxies can collide and merge together over millions of years.
  • ✨ The largest known galaxy is IC 1101, about 6 million light-years across.
  • ✨ Galaxies come in 3 main types: spiral, elliptical, and irregular.
