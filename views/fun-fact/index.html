Fun Facts About Galaxies

  • Galaxies spin like giant cosmic pinwheels.
  • We can see galaxies that are over 13 billion years old.
  • There are more galaxies than grains of sand on Earth.
  • Starburst galaxies give birth to thousands of stars at once.
  • Some galaxies are shaped like rings, tadpoles, or even X's.
  • Some galaxies contain supermassive black holes that are billions of times the mass of our Sun.
  • The largest known galaxy, IC 1101, stretches over 6 million light-years across.
  • Galaxies can recycle material, using supernova explosions to enrich gas clouds for new stars.
  • The Milky Way is part of a group of more than 50 galaxies called the Local Group.
  • Some galaxies are "dead", meaning they no longer form new stars and are filled with aging stars.
